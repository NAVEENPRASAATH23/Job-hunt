About SiliconQuest

SiliconQuest is a premium, interactive web suite custom-built to accelerate your semiconductor career search, streamline job applications, and refine your technical RTL design interview readiness.

It contains 5 integrated modules specifically curated for RTL design engineering roles, clock domain crossing (CDC) validation, static timing analysis (STA) concepts, and standard protocols.

Key Companion Modules

🔍 Silicon Matcher (Parser & Crawler)

Compare your resume against top-tier semiconductor profiles and scan live boards:

  • ATS Keyword Parser: Evaluates your resume for 43 key RTL skills (e.g. Verilog, CDC, LEC, DFT, Genus, AXI) and calculates a match percentage based on the job's requirement density.
  • Interactive Date Posted Filter: Instantly filter crawled jobs down to the past 24 hours, past week, or past month.
  • Automatic live crawler: Fetches live Greenhouse and Lever job postings for major chipmakers in parallel.
  • Direct Careers Apply Portal: Direct-to-portal link buttons let you bypass complex application routing and submit resumes directly.
📋 Job Tracker Kanban Board

Stay organized by managing your semiconductor job applications:

  • Drag-and-drop cards across pipelines: **Wishlist**, **Applied**, **Technical Round**, **Manager/HR**, and **Offer Received! 🎉**
  • Log key dates, compensation ranges, recruiter contacts, and technical questions asked in rounds.
⚡ Signal Architect (Interactive Timing Diagrams)

Interactive SVG-based waveform generator to visualize protocol states and clock edges:

  • Protocol Presets: Pre-loaded waveforms for AXI4 Read/Write Handshake, APB 3-Cycle Read Transfer, FIFO Flags, and 2-FF CDC Synchronizers.
  • Interactive Cycle Editor: Click grid cells to toggle states (0 $\rightarrow$ 1 $\rightarrow$ X $\rightarrow$ Z) or double-click to add custom text labels.
🧠 Practice Quiz & Flashcards Suite

Review high-frequency semiconductor technical concepts:

  • Flashcards: Study categories like Clock Domain Crossing, Digital Design hazards, STA skew, and Bus protocols. Mark cards as mastered to track your readiness.
  • Interactive Quiz: Test yourself on STA setup/hold, metastabilities, non-blocking assignments, and APB setups, complete with detailed explanations for correct and incorrect answers.
📁 RTL Reference Library & Companies Directory

Quick access to production templates and target company focuses:

  • Reference Library: Synthesizable RTL templates commonly tested in interview coding tests (e.g. Synchronous FIFO, 50% Duty Cycle Clock Divider by 3, Gray Counter).
  • Companies Profiles: Focus areas (e.g. branch predictors for AMD, interconnects for NVIDIA), locations, and interview difficulty ratings.
